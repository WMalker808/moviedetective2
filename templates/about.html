Movie Detective is a game where players try to guess a movie based on a series of clues. Each revealed clue reduces your potential score, so choose wisely!

How to Play

  • Start with 100 points
  • Reveal clues by clicking the "Reveal Next Clue" button (-10 points per clue)
  • Type your guess in the input field
  • Try to guess the movie with as few clues as possible

The game ends when you either guess correctly or run out of clues/points.
